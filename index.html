⚔️ THE THIEF TRADER RULE
Don't fight commercials. Follow their footprints.
Commercials don't speculate — they hedge real business exposure. An exporter locking in a USD price knows their order book. When they're aggressively positioned, it's because the underlying fundamentals support it. Their positioning reflects informational edge, not momentum chasing. When Large Specs are at one extreme and Commercials are at the opposite — that divergence is the signal.
